BLOOD DEATH KNIGHT — TALENT PHILOSOPHY

This page documents recommended Blood Death Knight talent configurations and the reasoning behind them. These builds are designed to emphasize survivability control, cooldown discipline, and predictable execution.

BUILD INTENT

Blood talents are selected to reduce volatility. The goal is not maximum damage or reactive healing, but repeatable mitigation, controlled Death Strike timing, and reliable cooldown layering.

MYTHIC+

Talent choices prioritize chained-pull stability, control tools, and mitigation smoothing across extended combat windows. Deviations are rare and should be deliberate.

RAID

Raid adjustments focus on encounter-specific damage patterns and defensive timings while preserving the same core philosophy: proactive mitigation and disciplined resource usage.
