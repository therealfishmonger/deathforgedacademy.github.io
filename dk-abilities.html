Death Knight Ability Reference

Core Death Knight Abilities

Death Strike — Core survivability and damage recovery tool.
Anti-Magic Shell — Magic damage mitigation and resource gain.
Death Grip — Positional control.
Mind Freeze — Primary interrupt.
Icebound Fortitude — Major defensive cooldown.

Blood Specialization

Dancing Rune Weapon — Parry and ability duplication.
Vampiric Blood — Maximum health and healing amplification.
Blood Boil — AoE threat and disease application.

Frost Specialization

Breath of Sindragosa — Sustained AoE damage channel.
Pillar of Frost — Strength burst window.
Obliterate — Primary rune spender.

Unholy Specialization

Dark Transformation — Empower ghoul.
Army of the Dead — Long cooldown damage and mitigation.
Scourge Strike — Core strike ability.
Mastery Is Proven.
