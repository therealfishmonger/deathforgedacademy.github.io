DEATH KNIGHT — ABILITY REFERENCE

This page lists core abilities, major cooldowns, and utility tools every Death Knight is expected to understand.

BLOOD DEATH KNIGHT

Death Strike

Primary survivability tool. Timing matters more than frequency.

Icebound Fortitude

Used proactively for predictable damage, not as a panic button.

Dancing Rune Weapon

Defensive and offensive value depends on proper timing.

Vampiric Blood

Amplifies healing intake; strongest when layered deliberately.

Gorefiend’s Grasp

Group utility tool. Poor usage creates chaos.

FROST DEATH KNIGHT

Frost Strike

Primary Runic Power spender. Avoid capping.

Empower Rune Weapon

Burst-enabler. Misalignment wastes damage potential.

Breath of Sindragosa

Requires strict resource planning to sustain.

Anti-Magic Shell

Used to negate damage and generate Runic Power.

Asphyxiate

Single-target control. Timing affects group flow.

UNHOLY DEATH KNIGHT

Dark Transformation

Central damage amplifier. Wasting uptime is unacceptable.

Apocalypse

Requires proper setup to deliver full value.

Unholy Assault

Burst window enabler. Use only when prepared.

Anti-Magic Shell

Defensive and resource tool when used deliberately.

Death Grip

Utility tool. Misuse disrupts positioning.
