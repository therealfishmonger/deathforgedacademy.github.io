Client FAQ

Read this before requesting services. It answers the most common questions and sets expectations clearly.

What services do you offer?

Deathforged Academy provides Diagnostic Log Reviews, Full Performance Reviews, and Coaching Reviews for Death Knight specializations.

Do you review logs in public Discord channels?

No. Public channels are for discussion only. All analysis and performance breakdowns are delivered privately to active clients.

What do I need to submit for a log review?

Character name and realm, specialization, Warcraft Logs link, specific encounters or runs, and your primary performance goal.

How long does a review take?

Diagnostic reviews typically take 48–72 hours. Full reviews may take 5–7 days, depending on scope.
