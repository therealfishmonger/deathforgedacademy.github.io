CLIENT FAQ

Read this before requesting services. It answers the most common questions and sets expectations clearly.

What services do you offer?

Deathforged Academy provides Diagnostic Log Reviews, Full Performance Reviews, and Coaching Reviews for Death Knight specializations.

Do you review logs in public Discord channels?

No. Public channels are for discussion only. All log analysis and performance breakdowns are provided exclusively to Active Clients.

What do I need to submit for a log review?

Character name and realm, specialization, Warcraft Logs link, specific encounters or runs, and your primary performance goal. Incomplete submissions delay the review.

How long does a review take?

Diagnostic reviews typically take 48–72 hours. Full reviews take 5-7 days. Coaching reviews are scheduled based on availability of both parties.

Does purchasing a review guarantee certification?

No. Certification is awarded only when defined performance standards are met. Services provide feedback and correction focused on improvement.

Can I ask follow-up questions?

Clarifying questions related to your review are allowed. Ongoing coaching, additional analysis, or new encounters require a new service.

Do you review other classes?

No. Deathforged Academy focuses exclusively on Blood, Frost, and Unholy Death Knights. Intentionally and unapologetically DK.

Can I get a quick opinion instead of a review?

No. Quick opinions create unclear expectations and low-value outcomes. Proper review exists to provide structured, and actionable feedback coupled with growth.

What makes this different from free advice?

Free advice is generic and unaccountable. Deathforged Academy provides structured analysis, evidence-based corrections, and clear standards.

Who decides certification?

Certification decisions are made solely by Deathforged Academy based on documented performance and defined criteria.

What if I disagree with feedback?

You are free to disagree. Certification standards and decisions are not negotiated but failures and areas of improvement are always communicated clearly.

Clarity first. Standards always. Progress is Key.
