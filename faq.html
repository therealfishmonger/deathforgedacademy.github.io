Frequently Asked Questions

What classes do you cover?

Death Knights only. Blood, Frost, and Unholy are treated as distinct disciplines.

Do you provide “best build” templates?

No. Builds are context-dependent. Execution quality matters more than static presets.

Are log reviews automated?

No. Every review is performed manually, with written or recorded feedback.

Do I need to join Discord?

Discord is optional for public access. Private feedback and follow-up discussions occur there.

Is certification guaranteed?

No. Certification is assessment-based and must be earned through demonstrated mastery.
